# Use Cases

## Marketplaces
Buyer funds escrow, seller fulfills, funds release on confirmation or timeout.

## Freelance and services
Client prepays into escrow and funds are released once work is delivered and approved.

## Property deposits
Deposits are held until predefined conditions are met.

## Logistics and shipping
Funds are released when delivery is confirmed.

## Ticketing and events
Funds are held pending event validation or successful transfer.

## Gaming and digital assets
Escrow reduces fraud in peer-to-peer asset sales.

## Recruitment and hiring
Placement fees can be released when a candidate starts or clears a milestone.

## Construction and trade work
Escrow supports milestone-style contractor payouts.

## Cross-border trade
Conditional release can substitute for slower manual payment protection flows.

## Financial services
Escrow can support conditional disbursement or payment guarantee models.

## Dispute resolution platforms
An arbiter can natively resolve and settle funds.

## Education and coaching
High-ticket course payments can be conditionally settled.

## Creative work
Escrow protects both client and creator.

## Vehicle sales
Funds can be held until inspection and handover conditions are satisfied.


## Revenue model

Typical monetisation patterns for these use cases include:

- protocol fee as basis points on release
- flat escrow creation fee
- monthly SaaS access for dashboard and API
- dispute-resolution fees
- enterprise setup and integration fees
